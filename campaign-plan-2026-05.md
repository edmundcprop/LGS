**Date:** 2026-05-06 · **Launch:** 2026-05-07 · **Initial test window:** 7 days · **Account:** lg-puricare.com.my

## 1. Campaign Overview

**Campaign name:** *LG Subscribe Search Relaunch — May 2026 (Learning Phase)*

**One-sentence summary:** Restart paid search with three tightly-scoped Search campaigns, phrase/exact match heavy, optimised against WhatsApp leads — establishing a clean conversion baseline before any scaling decisions.

**Primary objective (SMART):** Generate **20–35 WhatsApp lead conversions** in the first 7 days at a target cost-per-lead of **MYR 25–40**, while building enough conversion signal (target: 30+ conversions) to enable smart bidding by week 3.

**Secondary objectives:**
- Confirm which product category (Brand, Water Purifier, Home Appliance) has the strongest WhatsApp conversion rate, to inform week 2 budget reallocation
- Establish per-campaign cost-per-lead benchmarks for ongoing budget decisions
- Build a clean negative keyword list from the actual search-terms report (post-launch, week 1)

## 2. Target Audience

**Primary segment — "Malaysian household upgraders":** Adults 28–55 in Klang Valley / Penang / Johor who own or rent a condo/landed home, are researching premium home appliances (water purifier, air-cond, air purifier, washer-dryer), and prefer monthly subscription over big upfront purchases. They search in English with Malay/Chinese mixed terms.

**Secondary segment — "Brand seekers":** People who already know LG and are specifically looking for "LG subscribe" / "LG PuriCare" / "LG rent up" — these are high-intent and lowest CPA.

**Pain points & motivations:**
- Don't want to drop MYR 4,000–10,000 on a water purifier or aircond
- Want maintenance and filter changes included
- Prefer trusted brand over no-name subscription services
- Need help choosing the right HP / model — value WhatsApp consultation

**Where they are:** Google Search (highest intent), Facebook/Instagram (awareness — not in this phase), WhatsApp (the conversion endpoint)

**Buying stage focus:** **Consideration → Decision** only. We are not paying to educate the unaware in this phase — every ringgit goes to people already searching for "subscription" / "monthly plan" type intent.

## 4. Channel Strategy — Search-only this phase

**Why Search only:** Last 30 days, Performance Max burned MYR 1,928 with zero attributable signal — that's 35% of total spend with no learnings. Search delivered 2,288 clicks at MYR 1.56 average CPC and a 12.78% CTR (well above the 3–5% search benchmark), meaning the ad copy and keyword intent are aligned. Adding more channels right now will just dilute the conversion signal we need to establish.

| Channel | In phase 1? | Why | Budget allocation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google Search (this plan) | **Yes** | Highest intent, proven CTR, isolates conversion signal | 100% |
| Performance Max | **No (paused)** | Re-enable only after 15–30 confirmed WhatsApp conversions | 0% |
| Display / YouTube | **No** | Awareness channel — not the goal this phase | 0% |
| Meta Ads (FB/IG) | **No** | Different audience funnel — defer to phase 2 | 0% |
| SEO / blog | Continue (free) | Existing assets keep producing organic clicks at zero CAC | 0% |

| **CTR drops below 5%** in week 2 (creative fatigue) | Low — we have 12 headlines per RSA | If it does drop, swap 3 headlines per ad with new variants |
| **Conversion volume too low for smart bidding** (under 15 conversions in 14 days) | Medium | If we hit week 2 with <15 conversions, expand keyword phrase variants or raise budget on the converting campaign before adding bidding complexity |
