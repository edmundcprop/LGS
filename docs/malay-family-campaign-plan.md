# Malay-Family Campaign Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-06

This plan is built for:

- Malaysia-only targeting
- RM3,000 monthly media budget
- BM-first messaging for local family households
- WhatsApp enquiry as the primary conversion
- Google Ads only

## Objective

Generate qualified WhatsApp enquiries from Malaysian family households looking for:

- water purifiers
- air purifiers
- residential air conditioners

The strategy prioritises bottom-funnel Google search demand from BM-speaking family households and keeps the whole budget focused on high-intent traffic.

## Budget Split

- Google Search only: RM3,000/month

## Why This Audience

The strongest fit is not "Malay" as an identity target, but BM-speaking family households reached through:

- Bahasa Malaysia keywords
- family-oriented problem framing
- urban and suburban geographies
- monthly affordability messaging

That keeps the plan practical and platform-compliant while still aligning with the target customer.

## Core Positioning

Primary value proposition:

- premium LG appliances from a manageable monthly payment
- no large upfront outlay

- simple WhatsApp consultation

Family angles to lead with:

- rumah baru / pindah rumah
- anak kecil / bayi
- rumah lebih sihat dan selesa

## Google Search Strategy

Use search to capture active demand.

Campaign priority:

1. Brand defense
2. Water purifier
3. Air purifier
4. Aircond test

Recommended daily budget split:

- Brand: RM15/day
- Water purifier: RM55/day
- Air purifier: RM20/day
- Aircond: RM10/day

Brand campaign bidding:

- Use a `Search` campaign with `Target Impression Share`
- Set the placement goal to `Top of page`
- Start with `95%` target impression share on core brand terms
- Keep brand match types tight so the higher traffic focus stays efficient

Reason:

- In current Google Ads, `Performance Max` is an all-channel goal-based campaign type, not a brand-click campaign
- `Maximize Clicks` is a bid strategy, but Google specifically notes `Target Impression Share` can be useful for brand terms

Keyword rules:

- Launch with exact and phrase only
- Avoid broad match at launch
- Add negatives aggressively from the first week

Landing page rules:

- Keep traffic tightly mapped by product category
- Lead with monthly pricing and WhatsApp CTA
- Use BM-first copy where possible

## Conversion Setup

Primary conversion:

- `generate_lead` event from the enquiry flow

Secondary engagement signals to watch:

- `enquire_click`
- `whatsapp_click`

## Metrics To Judge In Month 1

Google Search:

- cost per WhatsApp lead
- CTR by ad group
- conversion rate by landing page
- search term quality

Meta:
Sales quality:

- percentage of chats that are real home/family enquiries
- quote request rate
- close rate by category

## 14-Day Optimisation Rules

- Pause keywords with spend but no qualified chat signal
- Shift budget toward winning water purifier terms first
- Add negatives from search query reports every 2-3 days
- Tighten geo coverage if low-quality traffic clusters by area

## Recommendation

Do not split budget evenly across all LG categories.

Launch the first 14 days with:

- Brand
- Water purifier
- Air purifier

Then expand aircond only if:

- enquiry quality is solid
- search CPCs are manageable
- operations can fulfill quickly

## Excluded From This Plan

- TikTok ads
- YouTube ads
- Performance Max at launch
